TLIC3004 - Drive Heavy Rigid Vehicle

Having an HR License allows the holder to drive a Heavy Rigid Vehicle – more than 8 ton Gross Vehicle Mass (no axle limit) +/- trailer (max 9 ton Gross Vehicle Mass).




TLIC3005 - Drive Heavy Combination Vehicle

An HC License allows the holder to drive Prime movers with attached single semi-trailer or Heavy Rigid (HR class) vehicles with attached trailer with a Gross Vehicle Mass (GVM) greater than 9,000 kg.



TLIC4006 - Drive Multi-Combination Vehicle

A Multi Combination (MC) class heavy vehicle licence allows you to drive any heavy combination unit towing 2 or more trailers, each having a gross vehicle mass greater than 9,000 kg



What Are The Benefits And Career Opportunities For
Obtaining A Truck License In Australia?

After acquiring a truck license in Australia, a range of promising career paths unfolds. Many individuals opt for roles as professional truck drivers, transporting goods for various industries, from retail to construction. Long-haul trucking offers adventurous opportunities, traversing vast distances and exploring the country’s diverse regions. Alternatively, some choose to work as owner-operators, establishing their own trucking businesses and enjoying entrepreneurial independence. Specialized transport niches, such as hazardous materials or oversized loads, become accessible with advanced licenses, opening doors to higher-paying and unique roles. The demand for skilled truck drivers ensures a steady flow of job opportunities across the nation.

About Us



Zaildar Training Centre is a proudly Australian-owned and operated truck driver training school, committed to delivering high-quality education and hands-on experience to aspiring heavy vehicle drivers. Based in Australia, we are passionate about helping our students become confident, skilled, and responsible truck drivers.

Zaildar Training Centre is dedicated to excellence in heavy vehicle training. Upon successful completion of any of our programs, students receive a nationally recognised Statement of Attainment, ensuring that their qualifications are valid and respected throughout Australia.


Why Choose Zaildar Training Centre ?

🛻 Experienced & Friendly Instructors

Our team of trainers is fully qualified, experienced, and approved to deliver professional instruction across a wide range of truck licence classes. Our instructors understand that each student learns differently and provide a calm, patient, and supportive environment to help you succeed.

📚 Personalised, One-on-One Training

We pride ourselves on our personalised approach. Each session includes one-on-one instruction, allowing you to build real driving skills and confidence under the guidance of experienced trainers.

🕒 Flexible Training Options

At Zaildar Training Centre, we understand that life can be busy. That’s why we offer flexible lesson schedules to suit your availability. Once you and your instructor are confident in your skills, we’ll assist in arranging your assessment at a convenient time.

🚛 Industry-Relevant, Practical Training

Our training programs are designed to reflect real-world driving scenarios, ensuring you are prepared for what lies beyond the test. We focus on safety, efficiency, and professionalism, so you can hit the road with confidence.


Please Note: To enrol in any of our training programs, you must be eligible to undertake training and assessment in the required heavy vehicle class. If you’re unsure about your eligibility, please check with the Department of Transport or call 131 156 for more information.
